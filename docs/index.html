🎯 What Is the Imperial Golf League?

The IGL is more than a game—it's a decentralized sports league where performance earns on-chain recognition. Players compete in seasonal tournaments, earn NFT trophies, and participate in governance through DAO voting.

🏆 Trophy System

Each season, players compete for on-chain recognition. Trophies are minted as NFTs on the Polygon network, with metadata stored on IPFS and tracked in our public registry.

📡 Live Events

Real-world tournaments are synced with the app and blockchain. Scores are logged, trophies are minted, and players climb the seasonal leaderboard.

📱 League App

The IGL app tracks player stats, syncs with wallet addresses, and allows trophy viewing, DAO voting, and season registration—all from your phone.
